Who We Are

The OMODARA Journey

From a Dream to Your Plate

OMODARA TASTY began with a simple vision: to bring the finest local and intercontinental flavors to every table in Nigeria. What started as a small family kitchen has grown into one of the most beloved premium restaurants in Lagos.

Our founder believed that great food is not just about taste — it is about the experience, the atmosphere, and the love that goes into every dish. That philosophy drives everything we do today.

We source our ingredients from trusted local farmers and premium international suppliers, ensuring that every meal that leaves our kitchen is fresh, flavorful, and unforgettable.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To serve delicious, high-quality local and intercontinental meals that bring joy to every customer. We are committed to using fresh ingredients, maintaining the highest hygiene standards, and delivering exceptional service that exceeds expectations every single time.

Our Vision

To become Nigeria's most trusted and loved premium restaurant brand, known for innovation in culinary arts, unwavering quality, and creating memorable dining experiences that keep our customers coming back for more.
