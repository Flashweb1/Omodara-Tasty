Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

You can order via WhatsApp by clicking any "Order" button on our website, or by messaging us directly at +234 907 949 0452. You can also visit us in person.

What are your delivery hours?

We deliver from 9:00 AM to 9:00 PM, Monday through Sunday. Orders placed outside these hours will be processed the next business day.

Do you cater for events?

Yes! We offer catering services for weddings, corporate events, birthdays, and private parties. Contact us for a custom quote.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash on delivery, bank transfer, and mobile money payments. Payment details will be shared when you place your order.

Can I customize my order?

Absolutely! Let us know your preferences when ordering via WhatsApp. We accommodate dietary restrictions, spice levels, and portion sizes.

Do you have vegetarian options?

Yes, we have a variety of vegetarian and vegan options on our menu. Check our Gourmet Veggie Bowl and Veggie Delight Burger.
